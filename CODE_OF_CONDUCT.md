{{PROJECT_NAME}} is committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free community.

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the maintainers listed in [MAINTAINERS.md](MAINTAINERS.md).

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behaviour and may take any fair and appropriate corrective action in response to unacceptable behaviour.
